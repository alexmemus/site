[Ogonek](https://russianburners.com/events/ogonek) is a local Russian Burn event — a smaller scale Burning Man.

Art is a big part of Burning Man DNA, but there are no museums there (as the whole place is a museum of sorts, and everything is burned at the end, so there is no reason to store something). To examine that notion I made a micro museum for Ogonek. Each room there represented one of the events (from 2017 to 2021), on the walls you were able to see participant photos from the corresponding year, and the number of viewers inside represented the participant volume that year. For 2021 (the ongoing Ogonek) there were no photos present and the viewers looked at an actual festival participant through the window.
