That was a street art object. I made a box with a question mark (as a Super Mario game reference), put coins inside and positioned it high on a wall. As most street art in Moscow it was destroyed the same day by a city janitor. Janitors don't earn much in Moscow, so that day he worked for nickels both literally and metaphorically.
